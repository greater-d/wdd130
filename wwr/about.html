Raft-X Nigeria

At Raft-X Nigeria, our purpose is to create an immersive and user-friendly platform that showcases the thrill of whitewater rafting, while making it easy for adventurers and families to plan and book unforgettable river experiences.

History

Founded in 2025, Raft-X Nigeria was established to bring the thrill of whitewater adventure to the heart of Lagos, offering unforgettable experiences for families, groups, and outdoor enthusiasts.
