Imo River Rapids

Experience the adrenaline rush of Class III and IV rapids on the Imo River. Perfect for adventure seekers who love fast waters and sharp turns. Safety gear provided and expert guides ensure a thrilling yet safe journey.

Otamiri Family Adventure

This family-friendly trip offers mild rapids, beautiful river views, and picnic stops along the Otamiri River. Ideal for first-time rafters, families with kids, or those seeking a calm yet scenic ride.

Idemili River Expedition

A multi-day rafting expedition for serious adventurers. Paddle through wild waters, camp along the riverbank, and witness Nigeria’s untouched natural beauty. Includes meals, camping gear, and safety equipment.
